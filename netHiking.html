⇐ FamOnTheWheels ⇑ Family Rides ⇑ Let’s Go Ride ⇒

The Net Hiking is a fun-filled and thrilling experience to the whole family where they have to walk along with an elevated walkway surrounded by nets.

  • Infants are not allowed
  • All children must be accompanied by an adult .
  • Individual weight limit is 300lb.
  • Persons with pregnancy concerns, heart, back or neck problems, or other serious medical conditions must not use netHike
  • Foods and beverages are not allowed
  • Net Hiking requires balancing capability
