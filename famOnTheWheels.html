FamOnWheels is a fun-filled family train consisting of different rail trucks. Upto 6 passengers are allowed aboard in each boggy!!

  • Infants are not allowed
  • All children must be accompanied by an adult rider
  • Riders must remain seated until ride comes to a complete stop
  • Riders should wear the seatbelts compulsory
  • Individual weight limit is 300lb.
  • Persons with pregnancy concerns, heart, back or neck problems, or other serious medical conditions must not use this ride
  • Foods and beverages are not allowed
