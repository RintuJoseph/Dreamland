Stay With Us, Chill With Us

You can maximize your enjoyment in Dreamland Theme Park by spending a weekend or two with us staying in our hotel. Our hotel is located inside the park and is the only hotel closest to our park. You can play all day long and then have a good rest in just a few minutes ride with our in-park transpotation. Or instead, you can have a romantic walk in the park under the starry night.

Besides, during the stay in our hotel, your hotel keycard is also your daily pass of the park! Each room comes with 2 keycards only, but purchase of extra tickets in the same invoice will be discounted by 50%! Please take good care of those keycards because any damaged or lost keycard will be charged when hotel check out. For details, please check with our staff at hotel concierge.

Buy tickets now! No need to queue up at the entrance

You can buy tickets here in the online store instead of queueing up at the entrance waiting for tickets. Tickets purchased here can let you enjoy a whole day in our park the same as tickets bought at entrance. However, you will never need to be crowded at the entrance anymore. Simply use your mobile and present the tickets bought here!

However, there is one major difference for tickets bought here. Tickets bought at entrance valid only at the day of purchase, but here, tickets has no expiry date! You can come visit us any day on your own preference.
