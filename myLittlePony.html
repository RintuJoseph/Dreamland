⇐ Merry Go Round ⇑ Kid’s Rides ⇑ Thomas & Friends ⇒


My Little Pony is a merry go round with colourful horses singing songs decorated with dancing lights.

  • Infants are not allowed
  • All children must be able to sit individually
  • Riders must remain seated until ride comes to a complete stop
  • Riders should wear the seatbelts compulsory
  • Kids with heart, back or neck problems, or other serious medical conditions must not use this ride
  • Foods and beverages are not allowed
